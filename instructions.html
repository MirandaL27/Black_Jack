Objective:

To get as close as possible to the number 21, without going over.

Card Point Values:

Each card denomination is assigned a point value. They are as follows:

  • Ace: can be set to 1 or 11 by the player

  • Number cards: value is the same as their number (e.g. two of spades = 2)

  • Face cards: 10

How the game starts:

When the game starts, the dealer gives you two cards, both facing up.

Then the dealer gives themselves two cards, one facing up, and the other facing down.

Player moves:

The player has two possible moves:

  1. The player may choose to hit, as in take cards from the dealer to get more points.

  2. The player may choose to stand, as in not take cards from the dealer and skip their turn.

Dealer moves:

  1. If the dealer has a point total of 17 or greater: They must stand

  2. If the dealer has an ace and counting that ace as 11 would put their point total at 17 or over: They must stand.

  3. If the dealer has a point total of 1-16: they must hit until their total is 17 or over.

Winning/Losing:

After the player and the dealer have made their moves, their point values are compared:

  • Whoever has a point total that is closest to 21 without going over is the winner.

  • If the player's total is over 21: The player loses, even if the dealer's total is also over 21.
